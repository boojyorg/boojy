Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Boojy, we believe in radical transparency and respect for user privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data (spoiler: we don't collect much).

Website Privacy

Data Collection

Current Status (Pre-Launch): Our website currently does not collect any personal data, use cookies, or employ analytics tracking. We take your privacy seriously and have intentionally designed our website to be as privacy-respecting as possible.

Future Plans: When we launch, we may implement privacy-friendly analytics (like Plausible or Fathom) to understand basic website usage patterns. If we do, we will:

  • Use cookieless, GDPR-compliant analytics
  • Never track individual users
  • Never sell your data to third parties
  • Update this policy and notify users

Forms

If you submit a feature request through our form, we collect only the information you provide (name, email, feature description). This data is:

  • Processed via Formspree (a third-party form service)
  • Used solely to respond to your request
  • Never sold or shared with other parties
  • Stored securely

Email Communications

If you email us directly at contact@boojy.org, we store your email address and message content only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. We never add you to mailing lists without your explicit consent.

Cookies

Current Status: Our website does not use cookies. No tracking, no authentication cookies, no third-party cookies. None.

Application Privacy (Future)

When Boojy applications launch, they will be designed with privacy as a core principle:

Local-First Architecture

All Boojy applications work offline-first. Your projects and files are stored locally on your device. We never have access to your creative work unless you explicitly choose to sync via Boojy Cloud.

Boojy Cloud (Optional)

If you choose to use Boojy Cloud for syncing:

  • End-to-end encryption: Your files are encrypted before leaving your device
  • Zero-knowledge: We cannot read your files or projects
  • You own your data: Export everything at any time
  • Self-hosting option: Run your own Boojy Cloud server

Telemetry & Analytics

No invasive tracking. We may collect minimal, anonymous usage statistics to improve the applications (e.g., which features are used most). This will be:

  • Completely anonymous (no user identification)
  • Opt-in or easily disabled
  • Transparent about what's collected
  • Never sold to third parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • GitHub: For code hosting and issue tracking (subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy)
  • Formspree: For form submissions (subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy)
  • Buy Me a Coffee: For donations (subject to their privacy policy)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of any future communications
  • Export your data from Boojy Cloud at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@boojy.org

GDPR Compliance

Boojy respects the rights of EU citizens under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we collect minimal data and don't track users, compliance is straightforward. Users in the EU have all the rights listed above.

Children's Privacy

Boojy services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Boojy services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Email: privacy@boojy.org
General contact: contact@boojy.org
