Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The Short Version

We don't track you. We don't sell your data. Your creative work is yours, and we can't see it. That's the whole philosophy.

Website (boojy.org)

What we collect:

  • Nothing. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels.
  • Your email if you sign up for updates (optional, unsubscribe anytime)
  • Your email and message if you submit a bug report

What we don't do:

  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Use cookies (none, ever)
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share your info with advertisers

Boojy Audio App

Local-first:

  • All your projects live on your computer
  • The app works completely offline
  • We never see your files

Telemetry (Optional)

Boojy Audio may collect anonymous usage data to improve quality. You can disable this in Settings.

What we may collect (if enabled):

  • Current app version and OS
  • Crash reports (sanitized - file paths and names removed)
  • Feature usage (which tools you click, not what you create)

What we CAN'T see:

  • Your projects or files
  • What you're creating
  • Any personally identifiable information

Your Rights (GDPR)

You can:

  • Request a copy of any data we have about you
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from updates instantly

Email tyr@boojy.org to exercise these rights.

Questions?
